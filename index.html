ABOUT US

Stone Chapel Kennel & Cattery has been a dream in the making for many years. Every detail of our facility has been designed with your pet's comfort and safety in mind. Our goal is for them to be as happy as possible while they are in our care. We opened our doors in January 2020. Please stop by anytime to tour our facility. We'd be honored to care for your pets.

Follow us on Instagram @stonechapelkennel and on Facebook at Stone Chapel Kennel & Cattery

Our Kennel | What To Expect

Here at Stone Chapel Kennel we promise to provide your loved ones with a comfortable stay. To do so we need a few things from you.

  • Proof of Vaccinations
    • Dogs: Rabies, DHLPP, and Bordetella.
    • Cats: Rabies, FVRCP, and Feline Leukemia
  • That your pet be on a flea & tick regiment
  • Food for your pet. Keeping them on their normal food causes less stress and will not upset their stomach.
  • Signed Pet Care Agreement
  • (Optional) Any of your pets favorite items from home.
