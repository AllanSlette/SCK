About Our Cattery

Our feline friends definitely prefer a quieter environment than their rambunctious canine counterparts, so we have a dedicated room entirely for them! Your cat will ENJOY a spacious split-level condo equipped with a cozy bed perches, and private litter box area. Your cat will ENGAGE in individual free time outside of their condo to snuggle with one of our staffers. Your cat will EXPERIENCE climbing the cat tower, playing with the room's many toys, and basking in the sun on the window ledge.



Required Vaccinations (Cats): Rabies, FVRCP, and Feline Leukemia
